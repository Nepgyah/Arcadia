## Stack
The project implements a stack of **Django backend** with **Next JS frontend with typescript**. The public website will also be on the Django side of the project while the platform will reside strictly on the Next JS side.

## Software
- Visual Studio
- Figma
- Obsidian

## App Breakdown
The app names are inspired by Japanese verbs
### Primary Apps
- Miru - Anime tracker and viewer
- Yomu - Manga/LN/Manwha tracker and viewer
- Asobu - Game/Mod library and store
- Kau - Merch and Goods
- Tsunagu - Social Media and Community

### Secondary Apps
- Iku - Event tracker
- Hiku - Gacha guides
- Shiru - Blog / Articles
- Kumitateru - PC List and Builder
- Kiku - Music streaming
